Why Sister exists

We were never
meant to do this
alone.

Somewhere along the way, we stopped trusting each other. We stopped borrowing sugar from next door. We stopped knowing who in our neighbourhood makes the best bread, or who needs a hand this week.

Sister exists to change that. Not through a feed or a following — but through real, local, reciprocal community between women who are done going it alone.

This is a place where commerce is in service of community. Where your first act is always a gift. Where every seller has made a promise to her sisters.

How it works

Three steps
into your
community.

Sister isn't another marketplace. It's a practice. A way of showing up for the women around you — and letting them show up for you.

01
Make your first gift

Before anything else, you give something to your local community — a skill, an object, your time, or your knowledge. This is how every Sister begins.

02
Join your local circle

Connect with women in your area. Share what you have, ask for what you need. The gift economy in motion — local, real, and ongoing.

03
Discover the market

Browse and buy from values-aligned, women-owned businesses near you. Every seller has taken the Sister Pledge and been welcomed by the community.

The Sister Pledge

A promise
we make to
each other.

Every member of Sister signs this pledge. It's not terms and conditions. It's a commitment to the women in your community.

  • i.I will be honest about what I have and what I need.
  • ii.I will not compete. I will collaborate.
  • iii.I will lift others up, not extract from them.
  • iv.I will show up for my local community.
  • v.I will assume good faith in my sisters.
  • vi.I will give before I take.
