Book

Braiding Sweetgrass

Robin Wall Kimmerer

A botanist and member of the Citizen Potawatomi Nation weaves together indigenous plant knowledge and scientific wisdom into something that permanently changes how you see the natural world — and your place in it.

"I read this and immediately wanted to give it to every woman I know. It changed how I think about reciprocity, about what we owe the earth and each other. It is the gift economy in plant form."

— Allison, Los Angeles
