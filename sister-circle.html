What makes this different

Not barter.
Not favours.
A circle.

Most platforms that claim to be gift economies are really just barter with extra steps — you give me this, I give you that. Sister is built differently. You give to the circle. The circle gives back. Not directly, not transactionally. That's what makes it real.

01
Give first, always
Your first act on Sister is a gift. Before you browse, before you ask, before you buy. This isn't optional — it's the door. It sets who Sister is for.
02
No direct exchange
You cannot respond to a gift with "I'll give you X in return." That's barter. You give to the circle. The circle — not the individual — gives back to you.
03
Needs are honoured
Asking is not weakness — it's trust. A woman who says "I need help this week" is giving the community something too: the chance to show up for her.
04
Story, not score
No karma points. No star ratings. Your profile shows what you've given and received as a story — not a number. Community is not a leaderboard.

Four ways
to give.

Launching in order of what costs least and gives most — starting with what every woman already has: what she knows. Goods are city-wide but pickup only — no shipping, no delivery, just two women meeting.

1
Launching first

Phase 3
Time
Your presence, attention, and physical help. The most intimate gift — and the one that builds the deepest community bonds.

Phase 4

How the circle works
1
Sign the Pledge
You agree to give before you take, assume good faith, and not compete with your sisters.
2
Make your first gift
Before your profile activates, you post something you're offering to the circle. This is the door.
3
Browse your city
See what women in your city are offering and needing. Filter by type, neighbourhood, or topic.
4
Give or receive
Respond to an offer, answer a need, or post your own. The circle moves because everyone participates.
5
Your story grows
Your profile builds a record of what you've given and received — a story of participation, not a score.

Goods · Special rules
Pickup Required
Goods are city-wide like all other gift types — but every listing is flagged with a pickup required label. No shipping, no delivery. The receiver comes to you.
Pickup required badge — always shown on Goods cards
General area field — neighbourhood or district, not full address
Full address shared only after both women confirm via message
Availability window — when can she collect?
One recipient only — first to respond, first to receive
Why pickup only
Keeps goods local. Builds real connection. Prevents the platform becoming a shipping marketplace.
Moderation
Community Trust
The systems that keep the circle safe and genuine — invisible when working, clear when needed.
Report a gift — goes to Sister moderation queue
Block a member — removes from your feed silently
First gift review — new members' first gifts are human-reviewed
No anonymous posting — every gift has a real name and city
Women-only verification — at account creation
Philosophy
Trust is built in. Safety is designed in. Not bolted on after.
